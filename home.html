About the Competition

Capture the world through your lens and showcase your talent in our annual photography competition. Whether you're an amateur or a professional, we invite photographers of all skill levels to participate.

Categories

  • Landscape
  • Potrait
  • Wildlife
  • Abstract
  • Street Photography

Prizes

  • Grand Prize: $1000 and a feature in our gallery
  • Category Winners: $500 each and a digital certificate
  • Audience Choice: $250 and a feature on our website

How to Enter

  1. Register: Fill out the registration form.
  2. Submit Your Photos: Upload your entries in high resolution.
  3. Deadline: All submissions must be received by 31 August, 2024
